About the Clinic

We provide expert hematology care for non-emergent conditions, focusing on anemia, leukopenia, thrombocytopenia, and coagulopathies.

Patient Eligibility

  • Anemia: Hemoglobin >8
  • Polycythemia: Hemoglobin <16.7, Hematocrit <50%
  • Low WBC: No less than 3
  • High WBC: No greater than 20, ALC <5 (if flow cytometry shows CLL, transfer to MD)
  • High Platelets: No more than 800
  • Low Platelets: No less than 50,000
  • Bleeding, Bruising, Clotting: Accepted except in pregnancy (see below)
  • Pregnancy Considerations: High or low blood counts after Week 12 of pregnancy (2nd trimester) follow the above guidelines
  • All Clotting or Bleeding in Pregnant Women: Must see an MD
