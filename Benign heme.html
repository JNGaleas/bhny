About the Clinic

We provide expert hematology care for non-emergent conditions, focusing on anemia, leukopenia, thrombocytopenia, and coagulopathies.

Patient Eligibility

  • Anemia: Hg >8, not symptomatic
  • Polycythemia: Hg 16-18 (Hct <50%)
  • Low WBC: 2-4 (ANC >1.0)
  • High WBC: <20,000 (CLL needs physician evaluation)
  • High Platelets: 450-1000, not symptomatic
  • Coagulopathy: Bruising, bleeding, thrombosis (physician evaluation if needed)

Diagnostic & Testing Guidelines

We follow evidence-based approaches to hematologic conditions with optimized lab panels and order sets.
